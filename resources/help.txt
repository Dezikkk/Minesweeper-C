========================================
           MINESWEEPER - HELP
========================================

                Controls:

-  W:       Move cursor up.
-  A:       Move cursor left.
-  S:       Move cursor down.
-  D:       Move cursor right.
-  Space:   Reveal the selected cell.
-  F:       Flag/unflag the selected cell.
-  Q:       Quit the game.
